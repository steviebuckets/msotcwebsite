About

Steve Martín is the host and DJ of My Side of The City. He's a lover of milk shakes, mixtapes and vinyl records. He lives and dies by the Chicago Bulls and never leaves home without his Blackberry. Founder of Culdesac Kids records, Web/Mobile developer in training and all around good guy.
